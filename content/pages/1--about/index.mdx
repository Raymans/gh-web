### About GeekHub

We are a bunch of software professionals based in the exciting city of Taipei.
Our mission statement is to recalibrate the way people conduct assessment this this modern day and age.
Each of us comes from different realm of the software industry, and we all have a common consensus that we can do better in online assessments.

GeekHub endeavors to bring professionals of all field together into this very online hub, and start collaboration with anyone, in anywhere!
